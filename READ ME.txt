Zenbox

Chrysasynth
Standalone Edition

Thank you for downloading this tool.

Zenbox is a breathing and focus instrument
from the Chrysasynth lab.

It guides slow breathing cycles while generating
immersive sound textures such as brown noise
and gentle binaural tones.

Designed for calm, focus, and meditation.

HOW TO USE

Extract the folder

Open "index.html" in your browser

Click "Start" to begin the breathing session

Follow the expanding and contracting circle

Adjust sound and volume as desired

Use the session timer for timed breathing sessions.

CONTROLS

Start
Begins the breathing cycle

Stop
Ends the session

Sound
Toggles the audio environment

Volume
Controls the overall sound level

PATTERNS

Box
Balanced breathing cycle (4·4·4·4)

Relax
Extended exhale pattern (4·7·8)

Focus
Steady rhythmic breathing (5·5·5·5)

Calm
Slow grounding breath cycle (6·2·7·2)

Each pattern uses a different breathing rhythm
to support relaxation or concentration.

SOUND

Brown Noise
Deep calming ambient noise

Alpha
Light binaural tone around 10 Hz
for relaxed awareness

Theta
Slower binaural tone around 6 Hz
for deeper meditative states

SESSION TIMER

Choose a duration for the breathing session
or leave it on infinite mode.

The session will stop automatically
when the timer completes.

COMPATIBILITY

Works in modern browsers

Chrome
Edge
Safari
Firefox

Desktop and mobile supported.

Note
Audio starts after user interaction
due to browser audio policies.
